2. Important Questions (Unit-I)

A. Long Essay Questions (8 Marks)

Write the summary/theme of the essay "Punjab di Atma".
Describe the "Rasman, Tyohar te Mele" (Rituals, Festivals, and Fairs) of Punjab.
Discuss the "Jeewan" (Life) of Punjabi people as depicted in the text.
Write a note on "Punjabi Kala" (Art).

B. Short Answer Questions (History & GK) (4+4 Marks)

Q: Why is the land named "Punjab" (Panj-Aab)?
Ans: Because of the five rivers (Sutlej, Beas, Ravi, Chenab, Jhelum).
Q: Name the oldest book in the world. Where was it written?
Ans: Rigveda. Written in the Punjab region (Sapta Sindhu).
Q: Why did King Ashoka become a Buddhist?
Ans: After seeing the bloodshed in the Kalinga War.
Q: When and where did Babur lay the foundation of the Mughal Empire?
Ans: 1526 AD, First Battle of Panipat.
Q: What does "Pentapotamia" mean?
Ans: Greek name for Punjab meaning "Land of Five Rivers".
